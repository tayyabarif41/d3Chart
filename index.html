Map

The first tab will show a map of the United States. The color of each state will depend on the total number of shootings. Each state can be clicked on to be zoomed. Once zoomed in, there will be markers showing the exact location of the shootings.

Bar Chart

The second tab will show a bar chart with the y-axis being the total number of cases and x-axis is race. There will be a dropdown where the user will be able to sort the barchart by ascending or descending order of cases.

  • Ascending
  • Descending

Line Chart

The third tab will show a line chart with the y-axis being the total number of cases and x-axis being the date. There will be lines to represent the ‘manner of death’ as well. There will be a filter (which can be a dropdown) that will show whether the police officer was carrying a body camera or not.

BoxPlot Graph

The fourth tab will show a boxplot graph with the y-axis being the age, and the x-axis being the gender. The boxplot will also show what arms were held by the people who were shot.
